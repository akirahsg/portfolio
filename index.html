NES Legend of Zelda Recreation and Custom Level

I recreated the first dungeon of the NES Legend of Zelda and added a custom level. I worked on implementing weapons, including the sword, bow, bomb, and a custom water gun. I also drew some sprites, added sounds, and implemented the animations for the enemies, Stalfos, and player movement, along with all collectables and the custom level.
